# **App Name**: ExamPrep AI

## Core Features:

- Subject Dashboard: Display a list of subjects and their respective chapters in a clear, navigable dashboard.
- Quiz Generation: Allow users to select specific chapters to generate a multiple-choice quiz with a defined number of questions.
- AI Doubt Solver: Enable students to input questions (text or image) and receive AI-powered explanations and solutions; use a tool to determine when or whether particular content should be added to the AI's solution based on question's specific features.
- Quiz Feedback: Provide feedback after quiz completion, indicating correct and incorrect answers with explanations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Blue (#3F51B5) to evoke trust, professionalism, and intelligence.
- Background color: Very Light Blue (#E8EAF6), a desaturated shade of the primary color, to maintain focus on the content.
- Accent color: Teal (#009688), an analogous color to the primary, for highlights and interactive elements to promote a sense of calm and focus.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for clear readability and a modern yet warm feel, ensuring ease of reading on all devices.
- Utilize Material Design icons for a consistent, clean, and intuitive user experience, focusing on representing academic and problem-solving concepts.
- Implement a responsive grid layout based on Material Design principles, ensuring optimal viewing and interaction across devices, especially mobile.
- Use subtle animations for transitions and feedback to enhance usability without being distracting.
